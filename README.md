# OIBSIP-CyberSecurity Task 6: Research Report on the Importance of Patch ManagementA systematic look at vulnerability remediation and why delaying updates is critical. 
Focus Areas: The life of a CVE, the enterprise patch management lifecycle, Service Level Agreements (SLAs), and virtual patching.
Highlights: Analyzes the catastrophic impacts of poor patch management through the Equifax Data Breach and the WannaCry Ransomware Pandemic, providing actionable frameworks for asset discovery, testing, and deployment.
